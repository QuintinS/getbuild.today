David Cross

Personal trainer and athlete David Cross has a mission — a mission to get people eating well, training hard and living life the good way. He has been featured multiple times in publications such as Men's Health, Men's Fitness and Sports Illustrated, but as yet still had no online presence.

David need a website, wanting to showcase his services and nutrition products online. He chose Build to fill that need.

A Simple, Effective Solution

David's reaction to using our editor was resoundingly positive — like many first-time users, he had the reaction of "I can't believe how easy this is to do." Within a couple of days of working on the site, he had his content laid out and working.

Build's editor is so simple, if you have ever used a word processor like Microsoft Word, you already know how to use it. It's very easy for someone who knows nothing about web design to create a gorgeous website in no time.

Services Page

The chief reason for setting up David Cross' site was to expose his products to a larger audience. David needed a page to show the pricing list for his products and coaching services. Creating the page was simple — selecting the page template he wanted took seconds. After that, it was just a matter of replacing the content in the template with the pricing information for his products.

About Us Page

When choosing a personal trainer, you want to know that you are choosing a person who has achieved his own personal fitness goals and who you can relate to.

The "About Us" page features a short profile of David and a writeup with his motivations for creating the site, and a breakdown of the services he offers.

Contact Page

When creating a website for your business, it's crucial to make it easy for your visitors to get in touch with you. Build has a built-in contact form that can be created and set up with a few clicks, and which will send contact e-mail addresses to your profile's e-mail address.
