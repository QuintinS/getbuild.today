Why SaaS is the way of the future

Ross Allchorn | 25 August 2014

In this day and age you have a huge number of options when it comes to creating a new website for you or your business. There are likely more website designers out there than there are dollars in your bank account.

You'll face an entire spectrum of website creators from the hip-trend-setting studio employee on a Mac Pro that consumes only the finest beverages made from pre-digested coffee beans through to the basement dwelling hermit that bashes away at a cigarette burnt keyboard and burns his retinas on an old 14 inch CRT monitor. The one will burn a hole in your pocket and the other will burn a hole in your soul. There are those somewhere in between, but whichever way you look at it, you're looking for a suitable match when it comes to both skills, price, speed of execution and whether or not you're able to work with the person.
